# SimRaceWatch 90-Day Editorial + Ops Plan

## Goals (90 days)
- Publish 30 search-targeted evergreen drafts (10/month).
- Stand up daily cron-assisted draft generation + weekly QA sweep.
- Keep voice grounded in practical lap-time consistency gains, not hype.

## Month 1: Foundation
- Finalise keyword clusters (pedals, wheelbases, rig stiffness, FOV, braking consistency).
- Ship first 10 articles (mix: best-of, setup how-to, versus).
- Add lint gate for: skip-if, trade-off, concrete scenario.
- Enable internal linking map between buying guides and setup guides.

## Month 2: Scale + Conversion
- Publish next 10 articles focused on mid-funnel buyer intent.
- Add 'where to buy' blocks with affiliate wrappers + click tracking.
- Introduce comparison templates for high-volume terms (load cell vs hall sensor, DD 5Nm vs 8Nm).
- Weekly metadata/title QA and sitemap validation.

## Month 3: Authority + Refresh
- Publish final 10 queue items.
- Refresh top-performing pages with updated pricing and alternatives.
- Add quarterly roundup and issue-based pillar pages.
- Review CTR/CVR and rework underperforming titles/meta descriptions.

## Guardrails
- No generic AI intros.
- Every page must include: explicit trade-off, who should skip, measurable use-case.
- Limit alerts to actionable failures (pipeline broken, build fail, high-confidence lint regressions).
