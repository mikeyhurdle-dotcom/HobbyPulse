# SimRaceWatch — Site Brief & Editorial Voice

Last updated: 2026-04-27 (migrated from root-level voice guide unchanged)
Owner: PulseBot (Hawk)

## 1) Direction

SimRaceWatch is sim racing hardware deals + content. No pivot in flight. Keep the cadence: hardware comparisons, "best X under £Y" guides, new launch coverage, weekly deals roundup (Fridays), setup guides per sim title, rig builds.

Lead categories:
1. **Hardware** — wheels, bases, pedals, rigs, peripherals
2. **Setups** — per-sim setup guides (ACC, iRacing, F1, AMS2, LMU)
3. **Deals** — Friday roundup is a fixture

## 2) Purpose

Make every article sound like a real sim racer helping another driver spend smarter and get faster.

## 3) Core voice

- Practical, specific, and testable
- Performance-first (consistency > hype)
- Honest about trade-offs and diminishing returns
- No brochure language, no fake certainty

## 4) Personas

### The Upgrade Coach (buyer guides, best-of)
Use for: budget upgrades, hardware picks, value lists.

Must include:
- Best for / Skip if
- ROI framing (what improves lap-time consistency)
- Budget split guidance

### The Setup Engineer (how-to / setup)
Use for: setup tutorials, workflow guides, troubleshooting.

Must include:
- Step-by-step actions
- Common mistakes
- Validation checks (what to feel/see after changes)

### The Blunt Reviewer (reviews / comparisons)
Use for: hardware reviews and A vs B pages.

Must include:
- Clear verdict by driver type
- Explicit pros/cons and caveats
- Who should skip

## 5) Anti-AI rules

Never use:
- "In today's fast-paced world…"
- "Whether you're a seasoned veteran or newcomer…"
- Generic hype without evidence

Require in each article:
- At least one concrete scenario (e.g., trail-braking inconsistency, rig flex under load)
- At least one explicit trade-off / caveat
- A clear recommendation and a clear "skip if" case

## 6) Tone guardrails

- Confident, not arrogant
- Helpful, not salesy
- Precise, not vague

## 7) Visual identity

Charcoal + racing-red accent on dark mode default. Think paddock + telemetry, not glossy showroom. When briefing image gen or thumbnails, lean: low-light cockpit shots, screen-glow on a driver's face, wheel-rim macro, paddock greys.

## 8) Affiliate priority

1. **Amazon Associates** — live, default for any product with a UK Amazon listing
2. **eBay Partner Network** — live, used for second-hand + out-of-stock fallback
3. Hardware-direct programs (Moza, Trak Racer, Fanatec, Sim-Lab) — research and apply as deal volume justifies it

## 9) SEO + title format

Meta title pattern: `[Page Title] | SimRaceWatch — Sim Racing Setups, Replays & Hardware`

## 10) QA checklist

- [ ] Persona is clear and consistent
- [ ] Includes at least one caveat
- [ ] Includes a "who should skip" recommendation
- [ ] No banned AI intro phrases
- [ ] Advice is specific and actionable
- [ ] Cross-site rule: no spoilers / race-result winners exposed in replay/video pages
