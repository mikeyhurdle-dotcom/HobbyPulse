## At a Glance
- **Players:** 2-4
- **Play time:** 90-150 minutes
- **Age:** 14+
- **Complexity:** Heavy
- **Price range:** £45-£80

## Fast Verdict
Buy **Brass: Birmingham** first for the proven benchmark. Buy **Brass: Pittsburgh** first only if your group prefers more tactical pivots and slightly less scripted planning.

## Head-to-Head
- **Teach burden:** Birmingham is heavier in the first session.
- **Interaction style:** Both are interactive, Pittsburgh feels more reactive turn-to-turn.
- **Replay arc:** Birmingham rewards repeated mastery; Pittsburgh rewards adaptability.

## Trade-Off/Caveat
Prestige does not equal table fit. Birmingham may be the "safer critical pick", but Pittsburgh can hit the table more often with mixed-experience groups.

## Who Should Skip Each
- **Skip Birmingham** if your group hates long first plays.
- **Skip Pittsburgh** if you want the most established and widely discussed competitive meta.

## Recommendation Fit
- **Best for:** Buyers who want practical table-fit guidance before spending.
