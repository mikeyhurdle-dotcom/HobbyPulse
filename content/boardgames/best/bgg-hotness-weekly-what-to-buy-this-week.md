## How to Use BGG Hotness Without Overbuying

A simple three-filter process:

1. **Table Fit Filter** — Will your real group play this in the next 30 days?
2. **Shelf Role Filter** — Does this add a new role, or duplicate what you already own?
3. **Price Discipline Filter** — Is this launch-inflated right now?

If a title fails 2 of 3, move it to watchlist.

## Weekly Buyer Framework (Works Every Time)

When Hotness updates, sort every candidate into one of three buckets:

- **Buy Now:** clear fit + fair price + immediate table time
- **Watchlist:** strong fit, weak price timing
- **Skip:** wrong fit even if everyone’s discussing it

This framework is how you build a shelf that gets played, instead of a shelf built from trend anxiety.

## Smart Budget Split for Trend Weeks

If you’re buying this week, use this split:

- **70%** on one reliable fit game
- **20%** on accessories/upgrades that improve play comfort
- **10%** reserved for price dips/restocks

That reserve matters. Many “must-buy now” games soften in price faster than people expect.
