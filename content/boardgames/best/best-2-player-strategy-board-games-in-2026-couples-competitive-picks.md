## Meta Description
Looking for the best 2-player board games in 2026? Here are practical picks for couples and competitive duos, with who should buy each and who should skip.

## At a Glance
- **Players:** 2
- **Play time:** 20-120 minutes
- **Age:** 10+
- **Complexity:** 1.8-4.1/5
- **Price range:** £18-£85

## If You Only Buy One
Start with **7 Wonders Duel**. It has the best mix of tension, replay value, and manageable teach time.

## Ranked Picks
- **7 Wonders Duel** — Best for balanced competition. **Skip if:** you dislike card drafting and denial play.
- **Patchwork** — Best for low-conflict puzzle nights. **Skip if:** you want direct confrontation.
- **Radlands** — Best for tactical card battlers. **Skip if:** swingy card draws tilt your group.
- **Jaipur** — Best lightweight value buy.
- **Lost Cities** — Best quick rematch game.
- **Twilight Struggle** — Best deep long-session duel.

## Key Caveat
At two players, rules overhead is more visible. A game that is "brilliant eventually" can still be a bad buy if your partner hates learning-heavy first sessions.

## Who Should Skip This List
Skip these picks if your table is usually 3+ players, or if you need fully cooperative play instead of head-to-head tension.
