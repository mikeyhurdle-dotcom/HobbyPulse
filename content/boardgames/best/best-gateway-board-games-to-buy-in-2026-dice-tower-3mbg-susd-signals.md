## Meta Description
Our 2026 gateway board game buyer guide: beginner-friendly picks that are easy to teach, good value, and still worth replaying after the first month.

## At a Glance
- **Players:** 2-6
- **Play time:** 20-90 minutes
- **Age:** 8+
- **Complexity:** 1.5-2.8/5
- **Price range:** £15-£60

## If You Only Buy One
Buy **Azul**. It is still the highest-confidence gateway recommendation for mixed groups.

## Top Gateway Picks
- **Azul** — Best all-round pick. **Skip if:** your group wants narrative or co-op only.
- **Ticket to Ride: Europe** — Best family onboarding game.
- **Cascadia** — Best low-conflict strategy step-up.
- **Carcassonne** — Best for variable player counts.
- **Splendor** — Best short session value.
- **Pandemic** — Best co-op gateway entry.

## Trade-Off/Caveat
The easiest game to teach is not always the one with the best long-term replay. If your group graduates quickly, buy one gateway plus one medium-weight follow-up.

## Who Should Skip This List
Skip this list if your group already plays heavy euros weekly. You need mid- to heavy-weight recommendations, not onboarding-first picks.
