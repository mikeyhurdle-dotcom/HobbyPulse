Our Q2 2026 crowdfunded board game guide highlights the most anticipated projects with practical back-or-skip advice, risk caveats, and buyer fit.

## At a Glance
- **Players:** 1-6 (varies)
- **Play time:** 45-180 minutes
- **Age:** 12+
- **Complexity:** Medium to heavy
- **Backing range:** £1-£180+

## If You Only Back One
Back the project with the clearest rulebook, realistic delivery window, and demonstrated gameplay footage. Hype trailers are not enough.

## Back vs Skip Framework
- **Back** when: gameplay loop is already shown, publisher has strong fulfilment record, and core pledge is reasonably priced.
- **Skip/Wait** when: major rules are still "to be finalised", stretch goals rewrite core systems, or shipping risk dominates total cost.

## Notable Q2 Signals
- Creator buzz is high around campaign-heavy projects and mid-weight tactical titles.
- The highest-risk campaigns are still miniatures-first projects with unclear solo/co-op balancing.

## Trade-Off/Caveat
Crowdfunding gives early access and exclusives, but you trade that for delivery uncertainty, late rule changes, and weaker returns if the game misses your table fit.

## Who Should Skip Crowdfunded Buys
Skip campaign backing if you are budget-sensitive, dislike waiting 12-18 months, or mainly want proven games with retail reviews.
