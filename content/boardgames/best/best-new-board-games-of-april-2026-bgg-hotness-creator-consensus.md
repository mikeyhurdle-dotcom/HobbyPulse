## Ranked Picks (April 2026)

### 1) Dungeon Crawler Carl: Unstoppable
**Who for:** Groups who want high-energy co-op with table banter and campaign-like progression.

**Why included:** It combines big personality with repeat-session momentum better than most new splashy releases this month. Strong hotness momentum, but also strong replay potential once the novelty wears off.

### 2) Brass: Pittsburgh
**Who for:** Experienced strategy players who want a tighter, modernized economic knife fight.

**Why included:** The decision pressure lands quickly, and buyer intent is high because many players are choosing between this and Brass: Birmingham right now. If your table enjoys intense planning and timing races, this is one of April’s highest-confidence heavy buys.

### 3) Endeavor: Deep Sea
**Who for:** 2-4 player groups that like interactive medium-heavy euros without 3-hour overhead.

**Why included:** Excellent balance between teachability and long-term depth. It keeps showing up in recommendation overlap because it’s one of the cleaner “serious game night” purchases currently available.

### 4) Lairs
**Who for:** Mid-weight players who want tactical choices without drowning in rules load.

**Why included:** Consistent creator praise for table flow and turn clarity. It fills a real gap between gateway-plus games and heavier strategy commitments.

### 5) Excursions
**Who for:** Groups that value quick setup, approachable strategy, and regular weeknight play.

**Why included:** Reliable accessibility-to-depth ratio. It is easier to table than many trend titles and still has enough strategic arc to survive repeat plays.

### 6) Nippon: Zaibatsu
**Who for:** Heavy euro players who prioritize economy engines, efficiency puzzles, and optimization.

**Why included:** Not for everyone, but for the right table profile this is one of the highest-ceiling strategy purchases in the April conversation.

## Quick Buy Guidance by Player Type
- **If your group is mixed-experience:** Start with **Excursions**.
- **If your group wants co-op energy and personality:** Buy **Dungeon Crawler Carl: Unstoppable**.
- **If your group wants serious economic depth:** Choose **Brass: Pittsburgh**.
- **If you want best all-round medium-heavy value:** Pick **Endeavor: Deep Sea**.
