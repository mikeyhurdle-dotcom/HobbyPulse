## At a Glance
- **Players:** 1-4
- **Play time:** 60-120 minutes
- **Age:** 14+
- **Complexity:** Medium
- **Price range:** £50-£85

## Verdict
**Worth buying** if your group wants high-energy co-op deck-building with strong table stories. **Wait** if you’re budget-first or allergic to occasional swinginess.

## What Works
- Fast momentum once everyone learns ability timing
- Strong campaign stickiness and session-to-session progression
- Big personality at the table, not generic fantasy filler

## What Doesn’t
- Rules edge cases can interrupt early plays
- MSRP is steep if you only expect 2-3 sessions
- Four-player turns can drag with analysis-heavy players

## Player Count Sweet Spot
Best at **2-3 players** where tempo stays high and discussion remains focused.

## Trade-Off/Caveat
You’re trading system purity for cinematic chaos. If your table demands tight deterministic balance every round, this won’t fully satisfy.

## Who Should Skip
Skip if your group dislikes randomness, rarely commits to campaign play, or only buys games once heavy discounts appear.

- **Best for:** Buyers who want practical table-fit guidance before spending.
