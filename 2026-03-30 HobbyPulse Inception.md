# Session Log — 2026-03-30 — HobbyPulse Inception

## What Happened

Started from a simple question: "Are there other industries like gaming that we could capitalise on using Smashd concepts?" Ended up designing an entire multi-niche passive income platform.

## Key Decisions Made

1. **Not a social network** — utility site, useful to one person on day one
2. **Watch + Deals + Live** as the three pillars
3. **Warhammer 40K first** — passionate niche, zero competition for our angle, great affiliate margins
4. **Sim Racing second** — high-ticket affiliate items (wheels/pedals £200-3000)
5. **Multi-vertical engine from day one** — each niche is a config, not a rebuild
6. **Next.js 15** over Vite — SSR/SSG critical for SEO-driven traffic
7. **New Supabase instance** — separate from Smashd, keep businesses clean
8. **Linear board on Tealium Tools** team with "Side Project" label — keeps Smashd board clean
9. **Target: £500/month** — requires ~40K page views across verticals
10. **Dark-first design** — the community lives in dark mode

## Niches Evaluated

### Tier 1 (Launch with these)
- **Warhammer 40K** — battle reports with structured army lists, model price comparison
- **Sim Racing** — race content with setups, hardware deals (highest affiliate AOV)
- **Mechanical Keyboards** — build videos with parts lists, switch/keycap deals

### Tier 2 (Add later)
- **3D Printing** — print timelapses, printer/filament deals
- **Bouldering/Climbing** — beta videos, gear comparison
- **Board Gaming** — playthroughs, second-hand game deals
- **Aquascaping** — dark horse, zero competition, expensive gear

### Tier 3 (Cautious)
- **Beauty/Skincare** — SEO saturated, product extraction harder, not our domain
- **Cooking** — massively saturated, low margins
- **Photography** — mature review space (DPReview legacy)

## Competitor Gaps Identified

- **Nobody aggregates battle report content with structured list data** — Listhammer does tournament lists only
- **No price comparison engine for Warhammer models** — buyers check 5-6 sites manually
- **No unified live stream discovery** — tournament weekends generate dozens of simultaneous streams
- **Sim racing has tools but no watch + deals combo** — Track Titan does coaching, SimGrid does leagues
